SAS UK

SAS UK is the United Kingdom branch of the International SAS paintball family, The team attends multiple events every year across the country. SAS UK focuses on coordination and teamwork to represent ourselves both on and off the field. The team strives to achieve the best in all of its players with new recruits having to prove themselves on the field to be considered for a position on the team.If you think what it takes to bear the mantle SAS UK consider getting in touch either via the above email or by clicking the button below to visit our facebook page.

ABOUT THE TEAM

Global reach

SAS was a paintball team founded in America under the name silent assassins squad, Today there are chapters of the team in countries across the globe with teams in most of Europe, Asia, South America and in 46 of 50 states in America. These teams consistently go across the globe to play side by side with each other in the field and SAS UK is no different. Last year members of SAS Canada came to play with the UK team as did members from SAS Romania.A key feature of this is when players move from one country to another, the SAS teams global nature means even if you cant play with your home team while in another country there will always be a team for you to meet, play and relax with.

Team and Family

SAS UK isn't just a paintball team, its a community and a family. Interaction with the team, the benefits and expectations don't end when you leave the paintball field. Joining SAS UK brings you into a tight-knit community of people with a wealth of knowledge and skills as well as connections past the team.Opportunities to play alongside other big names in paintball will become a regular occurrence and You will also become more involved with paintball with opportunities to marshall larger games and lead people into the fray. This brings a necessity for all players to be good team workers and skilled paintballers, provided you show these traits playing for SAS UK will be an experience you won't forget and will keep with you for the rest of your life.

Presence on the battlefield

Every member of SAS UK is a tried and tested paintballer who has excelled not only in a singular match but then performed to that standard another three times just to be inducted into the team. Some members perform so well during the first game in contact with SAS UK they skip the induction games altogether. This means that when SAS plays in a match we make a significant impact, for many teams SAS is a known name and one they have played with and would happily do so again. We are also recognised by hosts of events, many of our members have played critical roles in major events such as North V South, Speed weekend, Superheroes and villains, Diamond wars and more. SAS takes home many prizes each year and always contributes more than most to objectives at large events across the UK.

SAS UK Team History

The global SAS team was initially formed in America, quickly growing to have member and then fully developed teams in most states, as they grew in size the name became known to paintballers outside of the states and soon chapters began forming overseas to bear the title of SAS in matches across the globe.Today over 40 countries have a fully developed chapter of SAS with the most prolific being SAS UK, Germany, Romania and Turkey.

SAS UK is a long-standing chapter of the SAS paintball team with a stricter focus on smaller squad-based play and a tendency to engage in CQB engagements, the team initially started with a couple of small teams banding together to form the UK division of SAS, with initial recruitment the addition of chapters with new teams being created in different parts of the country. As the teams' size grew we formed a singular SAS unit to allow for greater potential during the larger games and have been growing both our reputation and skillset since. New players join regularly and the team is looking forward to an even greater 2018 with an ever-expanding roster of skilled and capable player to secure even greater glory on the field.

Our Team Members

The rank structure in the SAS UK team is Done by the colour of your shirt, A red shirt stands for a commanding offcier of a group or collection of players. An orange shirt symbolises a second in command or a "2IC" who takes over when the representing red shirt isnt avilable. White shirts are normal players who have earned a position on the team.
